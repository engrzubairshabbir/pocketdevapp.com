Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

PocketDev is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

PocketDev ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered mobile development assistant application developed by Consultways, Faisalabad, Pakistan. You can reach us at hello@pocketdevapp.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign up with email or Google, we collect your name, email address, and a unique user ID.
  • Usage data: Prompts you send to the AI, projects you create, and feature usage statistics.
  • Device information: Device type, Android version, and app version for debugging purposes.
  • Wallet data: Your credit balance and transaction history, stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve AI code generation services
  • To manage your account and wallet credits
  • To send important service notifications (no spam)
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance

4. Google Sign-In

When you use "Continue with Google", we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We use this only for account creation and identification. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, or any other Google services.

5. Firebase & Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — secure user login
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • AI API providers — processing your code generation prompts

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

6. Data Storage

Your wallet balance, projects, and settings are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. We do not upload your project code to any server unless you explicitly use an AI feature that requires it.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with service providers (listed above) necessary to operate the app.

8. Children's Privacy

PocketDev is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@pocketdevapp.com.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase Authentication's built-in security, and secure local storage on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@pocketdevapp.com
  • Website: pocketdevapp.com
